Lesson

Recall the weather over the past 24 hours. Was it sunny? Rainy? Hot? Cold? A bit of everything?

Now reflect on the same 24 hours - how did you feel? Were you happy? Angry? Excited? Maybe ‘four seasons in one day’ like the weather?

Looking at the weather over time, we see patterns. We call this ‘the climate’.

And if we look at our emotions, feelings and thoughts over time, we can start to see patterns too. That's what this programme is for. To help see patterns that are helpful and unhelpful to your health and wellbeing.

If ‘knowledge is power’, then self knowledge is self empowerment.

This course is designed to help you enhance your personal wellbeing with care, compassion and kindness for yourself.
